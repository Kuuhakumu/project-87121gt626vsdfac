## 🛠️ Tools to install (home lab)

- **Hypervisor:** [VirtualBox](https://www.virtualbox.org/) (free)
- **Attacker VM:** [Kali Linux](https://www.kali.org/get-kali/)
- **Target VM:** [Windows Eval](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/evalcenter/)
- **Home SIEM:** [Wazuh](https://wazuh.com/) v4.9.x (free, open source)
- **Packet analysis:** Wireshark
- **Cloud sandboxes:** AWS Free Tier, Azure free account
- **Notes:** Obsidian or OneNote (searchable — critical for open-book practical exams)
