# Beyond Entry-Level: Mid-to-Senior Specializations (Years 2+)

Once you land your first role and have 1–2 years of experience under your belt, the focus shifts from fundamentals to deep specialization.

[← Phase 5: Job Hunt](05-job-hunt.md) · [Hub](README.md)

> [!WARNING]
> **Avoid the cert-chasing trap.** Do not pay for 10 entry-level certs. Depth in one path + real hands-on work beats a pile of overlapping credentials. **Let your employer pay for advanced certs.**

---

## 1. 🛡️ Mid/Senior Blue Team
*Detection engineering, threat hunting, incident response (Tier 2/3)*

**Skills to develop:**
- Advanced scripting (Python, PowerShell, Bash) for IR automation
- **Detection engineering** — author Sigma and YARA rules; manage detections as code in Git
- Enterprise architecture: hybrid **Entra ID** security, Conditional Access, network segmentation

**Certifications:**
- **BTL2** (~£499 / Centri) — advanced practical IR, threat hunting, malware analysis
- **GIAC GCIH / GCFA** — gold-standard SANS certs, now with hands-on CyberLive format (~$949+ exam; *seek employer sponsorship*)
- **Microsoft SC-200** (**2026-07-28 refresh** adds Security Copilot/Sentinel Data lake) if you went Microsoft-heavy

---

## 2. ⚔️ Advanced Red Team
*Penetration testing, offensive security*

**Skills to develop:**
- **Active Directory / Entra ID attacks:** Kerberoasting, AS-REP roasting, Pass-the-Hash, token abuse
- Deep privilege escalation, DLL hijacking, service exploitation
- **Report writing** — the real value of a pentester is explaining risk to stakeholders

**Certifications:**
- **OSCP** (~$1,649, OffSec Learn One) — the industry's most-required pentest credential
- **PNPT** ($499, TCM) — realistic network pentest + report + live debrief
- **HTB CPTS** (~$210) — technically rigorous, respected for depth

---

## 3. ☁️ Enterprise Cloud Security Architecture
*Securing hybrid/multi-cloud at scale*

**Skills to develop:**
- **IaC security** — scan Terraform/CloudFormation with Checkov/Trivy before deploy
- IAM least privilege at scale: SCPs, cross-account roles, PIM
- **DevSecOps** — embed security scans in CI/CD (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI)

**Certifications:**
- **AWS Certified Security – Specialty** (~$300)
- **Microsoft AZ-500 successor** — *AZ-500 itself retires Aug 31 2026; check for the replacement before enrolling*
- **CCSP** (~$599, ISC2) — vendor-neutral cloud security

---

## 4. ⚖️ GRC, Auditing & Management

**Skills to develop:**
- Risk frameworks: NIST SP 800-30, ISO 27005
- Regulatory: **NIS2**, **DORA** (EU financial), GDPR, PCI-DSS, SOC 2
- Translating cyber risk into business/financial impact for executives

**Certifications (experience-gated):**
- **CISA** (~$575+ member) — gold standard for audit; needs 5 yrs experience
- **CISM** (~$575+ member) — security management; needs 5 yrs experience
- **CISSP** (~$749, ISC2) — the senior credential; requires **5 years** across 2+ of 8 domains

---

## The principle

Pick **one** track. Go deep. Build things. Let your employer pay for the $900+ certs. A focused specialist with a real portfolio out-earns a generalist with a wall of certificates.
