## What's Dated vs a 2021-era list
- A+ 220-1101/1102 → **220-1201/1202**; Sec+ SY0-601 → **SY0-701**; CySA+ CS0-002 → **CS0-003**; Net+ N10-008 → **N10-009**; eJPT v1 → **v2**.
- **AZ-500 retiring Aug 2026** — any guide recommending it without caveat is dangerous.
- CASP+ → **SecurityX** rename.
- **ISC2 CC** (2022) now established + DoD-approved — must appear on every beginner guide.
- **Google Cybersecurity Cert** (2023) now the dominant pre-Sec+ onramp.
- **BTL1** now 10,000+ certified — mainstream, not niche.
- OSCP standalone voucher gone → **Learn One subscription** model.
- CEH displaced by PNPT/CPTS/OSCP for real offensive roles.
