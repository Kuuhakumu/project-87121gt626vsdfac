# Career-match quiz meow

> 12 questions, about 2 minutes.
> grab paper or open notes and tally your letters.
> no wrong answers. answer how u actually are, not how u think u should be.

**how to score:** for each question, write down the letter of your answer.
at the end, count letters.
top letters point to fields to inspect first.

---

## 1. free Saturday, no obligations. u would rather...

- **A.** build something: gadget, spreadsheet system, little app
- **B.** take apart how something works or how it could break
- **C.** organize a messy system until it runs smoothly

- **E.** help a friend fix tech and explain it
- **F.** redesign something clunky to make it nicer

## reading your result

- **clear winner:** start there. skim that guide intro and confirm the day-to-day sounds right.
- **two-way tie:** read both fields in [what-is-each-field.md](what-is-each-field.md). pick the day-to-day and downsides u can live with.
- **lots of B, C, G, or H with zero experience:** those are often second jobs. Product Management and IT Management especially are destinations. consider **E (IT Support)** as a fast on-ramp, then pivot.
- **lots of E:** good news, thats the most beginner-friendly industry door.
